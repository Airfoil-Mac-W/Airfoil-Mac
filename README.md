## Overview

Airfoil Professional Suite is a complete wireless audio distribution platform with the multi-source mixing, remote control, and advanced synchronization needed for professional and enthusiast multi-room audio setups.

Audio effects apply equalization, volume adjustment, and other processing to the stream before it reaches the speakers, enabling tone correction for speakers in different rooms with different acoustic characteristics. Per-output EQ compensates for room acoustics without affecting other outputs.

Remote control through Airfoil Remote on iOS enables source selection, volume adjustment, and speaker management from anywhere in the house without returning to the computer. The remote provides the same control interface as the desktop application.

Instant On eliminates the buffering delay when starting playback to a new output, providing immediate audio when connecting to a speaker. The technology pre-buffers content to ensure gapless start of playback.

Airfoil Professional Suite is the wireless audio platform for enthusiasts and professionals who need comprehensive multi-room audio distribution from any source to any speaker with the synchronization quality and control depth that serious distributed audio requires.

## Performance & Integration

Airfoil is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Airfoil processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
